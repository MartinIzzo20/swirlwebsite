Transformers: The Last Knight

This movie gets a lot of hate, and for good reason, somewhat. Optimus Prime is barely in it and the villain is uninteresting. While I think these are both problems, the film has enough good moments to make up for it. Its an underrated movie, and the Transformers movies are always awesome. 5 stars

Interstellar

I think this movie deserves all of the praise it gets. The story is both entertaining and artistic, and the visuals are stunning. One of the all time greats. 5 rainbow stars
